This game will literally require users to place their phones physically into shapes. This game works really for only large groups where there are a lot of devices at play, minimum of 12 players (but allow less for initial early development). Split into 2-4 teams (minimum 5 per team), the TV will show the split of teams. Once the game starts, a shape or stack or arrangement will show on the screen for what the users need to make using their phones. Some of the possible options are:

- Stack phones into a pyramid - Once it starts, the TV will show that there has to be a pyramid made with the phones, and that each phone will have a number on it indicating its required position. The phone that is designated for the top of the pyramid will have a button on it to be pressed once complete (add a secondary confirmation press so that they do not accidentally press complete). Once the players have finished, the team will press the button on the top of the pyramid and their time will be shown on screen
- Make phones into any number of letters that would work - for example making an S works pretty well with 5 players. Each player has to tap the button on their phones once complete
- Make phones into a tower - Assign numbers to the players for which part of the tower they need to build

Some rules are:

- Players are not allowed to touch any phones other than their own
